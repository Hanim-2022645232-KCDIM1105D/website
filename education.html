University

About

When

I got accepted into UiTM MARA in Kedah, which is about two hours from my home. I’m currently pursuing an Information Management course here, which was my second choice in the UPU application. I chose this field because I wanted to avoid math-heavy courses, and this felt like a safe option for someone like me who isn’t very confident in math. I’ve been living in the hostel for five semesters, and the accommodation is quite affordable per semester.

Favourite Subject

My favorite subject during this program was Technical Support Services and Maintenance for Information Agencies (IMD163). I especially enjoyed it because of an Internet of Things (IoT) project we did, where we successfully built a distance detector using Arduino Uno. One of the biggest challenges at university has been presenting in front of an audience, but over time, I’ve managed to build my confidence. Making friends hasn’t been too difficult for me.

Achievements

One of my proudest achievements was completing both my IoT project and a C++ project where I created an ATM Machine simulation. Balancing academics and social life has been manageable, thanks to supportive friends who also make time for us to hang out. I’ve reconnected with some old classmates from secondary school and made some wonderful new friends during these two years. Anis, Natrah, Nina, and Qilah are close friends of mine—they’re kind.

Spots

My favorite spot on campus is the view from my hostel room, facing an abandoned lake. Initially, I thought university life would be challenging or unpleasant, but I was pleasantly surprised by how kind and welcoming everyone has been. Looking back, one thing I would change is focusing more on my studies and learning to complete assignments properly from the start. It’s been a valuable journey overall! I will graduate February next year!

Internship Placement


I completed my internship at the Faculty of Languages and Linguistics at Universiti Malaya, where I was placed under the Visibility and Internalization Unit. My supervisor, Cik Marina Ramlan, guided and supported me, especially in the area of marketing. The internship lasted for two months, from July 23rd to September 18th, and it aligned well with my field of study. I got to apply my Canva skills at work, designing slides and posters for various occasions.

The environment is warm and welcoming, and the colleagues are incredibly kind and helpful. Overall, it was a rewarding experience!
